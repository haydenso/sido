Automating the Economy with Virtual Work Environments

Mechanize is building virtual work environments, benchmarks, and training data to enable the full automation of the economy. Our digital environments simulate real-world work scenarios, helping AI agents learn useful abilities through reinforcement learning.

The market potential is enormous: automating labor could generate vast abundance, higher standards of living, and new goods and services. Our vision is to realize this potential as soon as possible.
